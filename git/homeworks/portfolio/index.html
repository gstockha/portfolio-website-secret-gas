About Me

While getting my Undergraduate degree in Psychology from the University of Kansas, I developed and designed an online multiplayer game in my spare time using GameMaker Studio 2, Node.js, Digital Ocean, and GitHub. Over the course of two years, with no prior training or knowledge, I developed an affinity for network infastructure and online back-end systems in a multiplayer gaming context.

I hope to utilize my existing knowledge on back-end network-driven systems in novel spaces and frameworks, while learning new skills and meeting new like-minded people along the way.
